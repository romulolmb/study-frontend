Tacosauros Mex

It's always taco night somewhere!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  1. Mix eggs, sugar and butter in a large bowl
  2. Spread in a non-stick dish
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for one hour
  4. Let sit in room temperature for 20 minutes
  5. Eat it and enjoy!
